Tune Up

The Obvious Next Step

For Tune Hotels, “Tune Up” was the obvious next step. As an international hotel chain, we wanted to explore avenues and platforms where language wasn't a barrier, expand understanding of us as an international brand through a singular but popular medium that touches on so many aspects of daily life – we chose music.

Music is something that everyone can relate to in some shape or form and is part of everyone’s life, including our guests. Working as an international language, the UK - with a rich musical history and home to some of the most iconic artists and bands, songs, trends and fans – will launch the “Tune Up” series that will eventually move to become a global celebration. Through this musical avenue, the brand will continue to reflect our core principles of being fun, young, outgoing, savvy with a fashionable and urban twist.

The senior management team have deep connections to a musical past, with our CEO alone having worked in the music industry for over 15 years, and is delighted to demonstrate the progression of our brand to connect with our core audience through the love and passion of music.

We are extremely excited to initiate a partnership with such an established brand as War Child for this series of events and overall contribute to their objectives and charitable goals. This springboard will allows us to further develop a year round awareness of constant activities, launching with a like-minded entity that has deep roots in music on both a local and global level. The collaboration will showcase new live exciting musical talent in four major cities; London, Brighton, Manchester and Edinburgh in November of the year and bring the Tune brand to life for all guests to experience first-hand.
