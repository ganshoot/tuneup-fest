Brighton Line Up

Komedia - November 18, 2013
44-47 Gardner St, Brighton, East Sussex BN1 1UN

YUCK

Yuck features Max Bloom, who formerly played in the band Cajun Dance Party along with former 'Yuck' member Daniel Blumberg. Critics have likened the band to bands such as Dinosaur Jr, Pavement, My Bloody Valentine and Sonic Youth..

CHAMPS

CHAMPS were unwittingly founded when Steve Champion brought home an old guitar found in a local charity shop as a gift for his musician son Michael. Michael started strumming a tune, his brother David joined in, it became a song they called St Peters, and it was so good they decided to try and write a few more. CHAMPS was the logical choice of name.

Swiss Lips

With their viral bodypopping video for ‘U Got The Power’, Manchester’s Swiss Lips are finding themselves stars sooner than they expected. The track, a gleaming fluoro slab of B-boy infused synth indie is infectious enough. But the cut together footage from 1980’s breakdance movies has given the track a life of its.They’ve created a sound like Franz Ferdinand shot through with the spirit of the Beastie Boys, is fuelled by nothing more than an admiration and obsession for pure pop.
