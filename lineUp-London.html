King Charles

King Charles doesn’t look like anyone you know, he doesn’t think like anyone you know and he doesn’t make music like anyone you know. How often, in these days of near fatal over saturation, can you say that? He Spent his youth reading and listening and thinking and playing. He has put a decent wodge of what he has learnt into a quite extraordinary record that draws down influences from Afro-beat, rock and roll, glam, folk, hip-hop and country.The heart of each song is lit up by joyous pop and the edges are coloured in by wild and wonderful ideas. If you are interested in songs with some spirit and you are the sort of person whose mind is still open to magic, wherever it comes from, then you will like it very much.
